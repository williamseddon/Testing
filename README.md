# Real House of Tarot — Deck + Reading Guide

A physical-deck-first companion for **Real House of Tarot**.

Most people arrive here by scanning the QR code inside the box. The site is built to:

- **Get started with your physical deck** (shuffle, cut, draw, place)
- **Search the full card library** fast (titles, characters, quotes, keywords, major numbers)
- **Use spreads as prompts** for physical readings — with optional **digital draws** when your deck isn’t with you
- Link out to the official **store** and **contact** pages

## Quick links

- **Contact:** `/contact` (links to `rhotarot.com/contact` and `info@rhotarot.com`)
